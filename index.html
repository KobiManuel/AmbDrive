What's AmbDrive about?

We make emergency medical services accessible for expectant mothers, quickly

We are a technology-driven emergency response platform where expectant mothers can request specialized ambulance services at the touch of a button. Our mission is to reduce maternal mortality rates across Nigeria.

Why we do what we do

We believe every expectant mother deserves access to immediate medical care when needed. Our mission is to reduce maternal mortality by providing fast, reliable ambulance services through a simple app that connects mothers with specialized medical transport in minutes.

Specialized care,
when minutes matter most

Every minute counts during maternal emergencies. Our fleet of specially equipped ambulances includes trained midwives and paramedics who understand the unique needs of expectant mothers. With AmbDrive, help is always just a tap away.

Our app is available for download on all platforms

Whether you're an expectant mother, family member, or healthcare provider, our easy-to-use app allows you to summon specialized maternal care with just a few taps. Track your ambulance in real-time and share important medical information ahead of arrival.
